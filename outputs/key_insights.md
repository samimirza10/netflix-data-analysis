# Key Insights

## Overview
This file summarizes the key findings from the Netflix Data Analysis project.

## Insights

- Movies make up the majority of Netflix's content library.
- TV-MA is the most common content rating.
- Netflix experienced significant growth in content additions between 2016 and 2019.
- The United States and India are the top content-producing countries.
- International Movies and Dramas are among the most popular genres.
- Most TV shows consist of only one season.
- Most movies have a runtime between 80 and 120 minutes.

## Conclusion
Netflix's catalog is heavily focused on movies and mature audiences, with strong contributions from the United States and India. The platform experienced rapid expansion in the late 2010s and offers a diverse range of international content.
